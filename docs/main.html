About Me

I am a fourth year Ph.D. candidate in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at the University of Illinois Chicago. I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Shuo Han. I received my bachelors in Department of Mathematics at the Southern University of Science and Technology.

My primary research interests lie in reinforcement learning and game theory. Currently, I am working on model-based RL with ensemble method. Previously, I have worked on projects combining both model-based and model-free approaches and have examined optimization techniques for Stackelberg games.

This website is built in TeXmacs, you can access the source repository on GitHub.
