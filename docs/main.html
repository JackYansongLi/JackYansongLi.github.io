About Me

I am a fourth year Ph.D. student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at the University of Illinois Chicago. I am very fortunate to be csadvised by Prof. Shuo Han. I received my bachalors in Department of Mathematics at the Southern University of Science and Technology.

My primary research interests lie in multi-agent reinforcement learning and game theory, particularly in decentralized multi-agent reinforcement learning (decentralized MARL) and human-computer interaction domains. Previously, I have worked on projects combining both model-based and model-free approaches and have examined optimization techniques for Stackelberg games.

This website is built in TeXmacs, you can access the source repository on GitHub.
